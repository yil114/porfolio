About Me

UX Reseracher & Project Manager

My passion for healthcare design began when I first joined the UCSD Design Lab - Diabetes Design Initiative. I realized that both the diabetes field and the healthcare field, in general, are quite extensive. I enjoyed engaging with different stakeholders, such as healthcare providers, educators, and doctors, to gain insights from various perspectives. I am eager to dedicate more time to learning about this field and contributing to the greater good by designing for public health. Currently, I am working as a Research Assistant at the UCSD ReCODE Health, where my focus is on ethical education in the realm of digital health design.
